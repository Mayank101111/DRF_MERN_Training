Democratizing
Learning

Skillfy, an initiative of Dr. Reddy’s Foundation, is driven by the goal of making quality learning accessible to all, anytime and anywhere. To enable learners, enhance their knowledge and thinking, irrespective of social or economic status, we have incorporated the following distinct features in our platform:

Economical

In line with Dr. Reddy’s Foundation’s mission of improving the education outcomes of people from low-income households, we have made a majority of courses available at free of cost. Everyone can start learning, without barriers to cost.

Accessible

pWith a bunch of distinct features like font selection, color schema, transcripts, voice search, subtitles, and local language content, Skillfy caters to a wide range of audience and makes learning accessible specially to People with Disabilities.

Convenient

Creating course content for Skillfy is easy. Individuals can create start creating courses, in their areas of expertise, in local languages. Our teams will guide educators throughout to help them create quality content.
